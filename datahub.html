Methodology

1. Official IPC Benchmark

IPC phase is treated as the official reference classification. It is not combined directly into the behavioural stress index to avoid double counting.

2. Behavioural Stress Index


Behavioural Stress =
(FCS Poor + HDDS Crisis + HHS Crisis +
Livelihood Coping Crisis + rCSI Crisis) / 5

3. IPC Gap Logic

The IPC gap identifies whether household behavioural evidence is aligned with, higher than, or lower than the expected stress range for the official IPC phase. This helps identify residual vulnerability requiring field verification or prioritisation discussion.

4. Evidence Products

The DataHub produces: IPC gap records, population-group vulnerability summaries, and gap-status classifications.

5. Donor Use

This platform supports evidence-based prioritisation without assigning unsupported budgets or unreferenced financing claims. It is a diagnostic tool for donor dialogue and programme review.
