Data Use and Disclaimer

This dashboard does not reproduce or redistribute restricted raw datasets. All original data sources remain the property of their producers. IDDR’s contribution is limited to data cleaning, transformation, scoring, classification, visualization, and interpretation.

Methodology

The dashboard uses derived scores ranging from 0 to 100. Higher scores indicate higher vulnerability or pressure. The overall vulnerability score is calculated using a weighted composite index: 

Overall Vulnerability Score =
  0.20 × IDP Burden Score
+ 0.20 × Dryland Stress Score
+ 0.20 × Food Security Pressure Score
+ 0.15 × Nutrition Risk Score
+ 0.15 × Women and Children Vulnerability Score
+ 0.10 × Pastoral Mobility Disruption Score

Priority levels are classified as follows: Severe ≥ 75, High 60–74, Moderate 40–59, and Low below 40. These thresholds can be revised following technical validation with government and sector experts.
